The Solution: Real-Time Verification

           

ClaimCheckAI listens, understands, and retrieves evidence from your single source of truth the moment a claim is made. No more doubt, just data-driven clarity.

           
               
                   
                        1.                        
                           

Identify Key Claims

                           

Our ASR and Claim Extractor agents actively listen to conversations and pinpoint verifiable facts and data points mentioned during the meeting.

                       
                   
                   
                        2.                        
                           

Query Your Knowledge Base

                           

The Retriever Agent instantly checks the claim against your secure, internal data (Confluence, Salesforce) and trusted external sources using IBM watsonx embeddings.

                       
                   
                   
                        3.                        
                           

Deliver Instant Verdicts

                           

The Verifier Agent delivers a simple, unobtrusive report with a verdict (Supported ✅, Refuted ❌) and the source, right in your meeting window.
